# Sales for Founders

**AI prompts, templates, and playbooks for founders who have to sell but never learned how.**

You built the product. Now you have to sell it, and nobody taught you. This repo is the sales training you skipped: battle-tested prompts for Claude and ChatGPT, copy-paste templates, and playbooks written from 12 years of doing B2C and B2B sales, managing sales teams, and closing deals the hard way before AI made half of it easier.

No fluff, no "always be closing" clichés. Just the stuff that works.

## How to use the prompts

1. Open any file in [prompts/](./prompts/)
2. Copy the prompt into Claude or ChatGPT
3. Replace everything in `[brackets]` with your details
4. The more context you give about your product and prospect, the better the output

**One rule:** never send AI output without reading it. AI drafts, you decide. Prospects can smell unedited AI from the subject line.
